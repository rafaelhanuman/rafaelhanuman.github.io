**8. EMBROIDERY AND JACQUARD
DESIGN BY COMPUTER**


**Skills:**

- Proficiency in Wilcom Embroidery Studio application.

- Knowledge of Tajima Embroidery application.

- Knowledge of RichPeace Embroidery application.

- Programming of complex embroidery at the level of photography.

- Programming of multicolor embroidery without nodes.

- Programming of embroidery with minimal machine stops.



**Comment:**

Embroidery is an important topic for the Western textile industry because it urgently seeks innovation. It is a complex process because embroidery is expensive, the West is in an economic crisis, and Western countries do not have a strong tradition in this textile technique.

My learning of computerized embroidery took place through a course I requested within the textile industry. At that time, there were not enough interested individuals to sustain a regular course in a formal school.

When I tried the Chinese software RichPeace Embroidery, I found it was based on Wilcom Studio, with which I have extensive experience.
