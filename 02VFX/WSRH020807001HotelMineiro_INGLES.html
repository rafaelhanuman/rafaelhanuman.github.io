HOTEL MINEIRO 2012

2012 . MINAS GERAIS HOTEL ADVERTISING VIDEO

I didn’t finish this little video because I traded it for a few nights while I was in the countryside of Minas Gerais.

The manager was very friendly, but the owner was a real pain, as we say in Brazil.

The manager was doing everything she could to promote the little hotel, and the old man didn’t want her to promote the hotel.

The employees didn’t understand the reason, but I know that it stayed the way it is, and that’s it.

Most people have problems, problems they try to solve with available resources.

If an old man can no longer attract women, the resource he has is harassing the employees at the hotel.

Thus, we often see a professional who should have a certain posture, but instead has the complete opposite posture.

This little video you see when clicking on the image is yet another work that was left unfinished, but the story behind the work is interesting.

In general, the state of Minas Gerais doesn’t function properly. When I leave Santa Catarina to go there, it feels like I’m going back 20 to 40 years in time.

The interesting part of this work is that I did it with a smartphone. It was supposed to be redone later, but it didn’t happen, that’s fine. I didn’t think I’d be able to do everything with a phone.
